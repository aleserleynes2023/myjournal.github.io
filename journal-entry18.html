📔 Journal Entry #18

Date: May 17, 2025 – 11:37 A.M.

Letting Go, Lifting Up

Dear Journal,

Maybe… with the help of God, I can endure all of this pain without having to carry it alone.
Maybe it’s time I truly surrender every thought, every emotion, every weight that’s been crushing my heart. I need to hand it over to Him, because I know I can't do this by my strength anymore.

I’m beginning to realize something now: I should stop expecting things in return for what I give.
Love doesn’t keep a score. Care doesn’t demand recognition. I’ve been forcing so much, hoping for something to go my way when maybe the real answer is learning to let go.

Whatever happens… happens.
And if the path ahead means sacrifice, then maybe that’s what I need to do not out of weakness, but out of love. Not to lose myself, but to finally find peace in knowing I tried, and I loved, without conditions.

Lord, please guide me. I’m letting go not of the love I carry but of the weight I’ve held too long. Help me trust in Your plan more than my own.
