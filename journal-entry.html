📔 Journal Entry #1

Date: May 13, 2025

The Space Between Healing and Missing You

Dear Journal,

Today marks a day since I asked her for space. And honestly, it’s strange. There’s this quiet ache in me that hasn’t gone away. I still feel like something’s missing like part of me is still reaching for her. But deep down, I believe stepping back is the right thing. I need this time… to find myself again. To heal.

I’ve been through a lot lately. I’ve felt weak, broken, and ashamed of not being as strong as I hoped I would be. But each day, I keep pushing forward, even if it’s hard. There are thoughts in my head I can’t control clouds I can’t shake off. I think I’m still searching for peace. And the truth is, I’m doubting myself more than I ever have.

Exams are approaching, and I don’t even know if I’m ready. Everything feels so heavy, and I’m exhausted physically, emotionally, spiritually. I used to be able to count on myself, but lately, I just feel tired. Like the strength I once had is slowly fading.

More than anything, I want to reconnect with God. I miss that part of me the one who found comfort and identity in faith. I want to find myself again, through Him. Maybe that’s where healing will begin.

Despite everything, I still miss her. And despite every pain that lives inside me even those nights I cried over her I would still choose her. Again and again. Because I really, deeply love her.

—Aleser
