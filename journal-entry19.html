📔 Journal Entry #19

Date: May 17, 2025 – 2:56 P.M.

Is It Too Much to Feel?

Dear Journal,

Are my feelings valid?
Is there truly a reason why I’ve felt all of these things in just a few days waves of pain, loneliness, confusion and now I feel like I don’t belong anywhere? Like I’m just an outcast slowly drifting further away?

Ever since I began to isolate myself, I started to wonder… who would even notice? Who would check in and ask if I’m okay, or if something’s wrong? Maybe it’s selfish to expect that so I stay silent. I don’t bother anyone anymore… not even her.

I love her deeply, but whenever I try to explain what’s inside me, it just ends up messy. Telling the truth feels like lighting a fire. And maybe… being me has always been messy. That’s why I’m tired of opening up. I’m tired of making things worse just by being real.

So maybe from now on, I’ll keep everything to myself.
If I have to walk alone, then I’ll walk alone.
If I have to grow alone, then I’ll grow alone.
Until the day comes when I can finally say I’ve learned how to live without needing to be understood.

But still… a small part of me hopes someone would understand anyway.
