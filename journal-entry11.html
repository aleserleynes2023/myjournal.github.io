📔 Journal Entry #11

Date: May 15, 2025 – 10:23 P.M.

Delivered, But Never Received

Dear Journal,

There she was active. My message got delivered.
Still… no reply. Not even a single word.

And just like that, everything inside me began to crack again.

I don’t even know how to explain what I’m feeling anymore in a way that makes sense. It’s like I’m watching myself fall apart in slow motion, helpless to stop it. I keep reaching out, holding on to the smallest hope, but every time I get no response, it feels like I’m being told without words that I don’t matter.

I’m starting to fall back into that space I promised myself I’d climb out of.
And what hurts is that I’m not even angry. I’m just… exhausted.
Exhausted from hoping. From trying. From wondering if I ever meant something real.

Sometimes, silence screams louder than words ever could.
And tonight, that silence is deafening.

—Aleser
